Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring kids or a date?

We love your family and friends, but due to space constraints, we are only able to accommodate those guests formally invited on your invitation. If you have questions about your invitation, please reach out to us directly.

Will the wedding be outdoors?

The venue is largely indoors, so you won't need to worry about the weather! However, there are beautiful outdoor areas available for photography if you'd like to take photos outside.

When do I need to RSVP by?

Please submit your RSVP by February 1, 2027. This helps us finalize our guest count with the venue and caterers. We appreciate your timely response!

What time should I arrive?

Please plan to arrive by 5:00 PM. The ceremony will begin promptly at 5:30 PM, and we want to make sure everyone is seated and ready to celebrate!

Is everything in the same location?

Yes! Both the ceremony and reception will take place at The Brix on Fox. You won't need to travel between venues - everything you need for the evening is in one beautiful location.

How do I get in touch with you?

For any questions or concerns, feel free to text either Blythe or Anson directly. We're happy to help with any questions you may have about the wedding!

How do I get to and from the venue?

We will provide shuttles from the Chicago Marriott Northwest hotel to the venue and back. If you prefer, Uber and Lyft are also readily available in the area. See our Travel page for more details.
