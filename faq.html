Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring kids or a date?

We are only able to accommodate those guests named on your invitation. Feel free to reach out with further questions!

Will the wedding be outdoors?

The venue is largely indoors, so you won't need to worry about the weather! However, the venue is along a river and some outdoor areas available for photography if you'd like to take photos outside. The weather in April in Chicago can be unpredictable, so pack accordingly!

When do I need to RSVP by?

Please submit your RSVP by February 1, 2027. This helps us finalize our guest count with the venue and caterers. We appreciate your timely response!

What time should I arrive?

Please plan to arrive by 4:30 PM. The ceremony will begin promptly at 5:00 PM, and we want to make sure everyone is seated and ready to celebrate!

Is everything in the same location?

Both the ceremony and reception will take place at The Brix on Fox.

How do I get in touch with you?

For any questions or concerns, feel free to text either Blythe or Anson directly. We're happy to help with any questions you may have about the wedding!

What is the dress code?

For the welcome drinks, come as you are! For the wedding and reception, the dress code is formal.

How do I get to and from the venue?

We will provide shuttles from the Chicago Marriott Northwest hotel to the venue and back. Parking is available at the venue if you do rent a car, and if you prefer, Uber and Lyft are also readily available in the area. See our Travel page for more details.
