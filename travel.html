Travel Information

✈

Flying In

O'Hare International Airport (ORD)

We recommend flying into O'Hare International Airport (ORD) for the most convenient access to Carpentersville. This is Chicago's main international hub with flights from around the world.

Distance to venue: ~25 miles / 40 minutes

Alternative: Midway International Airport (MDW)

Chicago Midway is another option, though it's a bit farther from Carpentersville. It often has good deals on domestic flights.

Distance to venue: ~40 miles / 55 minutes

⚲

Ground Transportation

From the Airport to Hotel

Uber/Lyft: The easiest option. Simply request a ride to the Chicago Marriott Northwest in Hoffman Estates. Cost is typically $35-50 from O'Hare, $60-80 from Midway.

No car needed! You won't need to rent a car for your stay. The hotel is close to the venue, and we're providing shuttle service.
